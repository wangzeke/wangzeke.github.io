Zeke Wang (王则可)

Research Profile

My current research topic is to accelerate Machine Learning training in any computing platforms, with a focus on the FPGA-enhanced computation and communication.

Computation: I propose a one-size-fits-all framework (MLWeaving) that allows any-precision linear model training on FPGAs, as well as any-precision K-Means.

Communication: on-going. I believe FPGA is a nice alternative platform for the communication of Machine Learning training. Keep updated...

Previously I have been working on programming FPGAs with high-level languages, with a focus on OpenCL.
